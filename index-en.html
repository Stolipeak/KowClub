About KOW

KOW invites you to discover authentic Cambodian street food in a unique cyberpunk atmosphere. Our chef, trained in Phnom Penh, reinvents traditional flavors while respecting the authenticity of ancestral recipes.

In our restaurant, we merge Cambodia's culinary heritage with a modern and immersive experience, creating an unforgettable gastronomic journey in the heart of Paris.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your dishes halal certified?

Yes, all our dishes are halal certified. We are committed to maintaining halal standards in our kitchen.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we deliver through Deliveroo. You can order directly from our Deliveroo page to enjoy our dishes at home.

Can I book for a group?

Yes, we accept group bookings. For groups of more than 6 people, please contact us directly.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes, we offer several vegetarian options including our Buddha Bowl and other suitable dishes.
